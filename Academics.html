Our History

Mahati boasts state-of-the-art Science labs, computer labs, well-stocked libraries, and multimedia facilities, providing our students with access to the latest tools and resources. We offer a diverse range of sports and co-curricular activities to our students, including cricket, badminton, gymnastics, debating, classical dance, and classical music, among others. Our school is committed to promoting an environment of holistic development, and we take pride in the numerous clubs we have established, such as the Language Club, Math Club, Sports Club, STEM Lab, Dance Club, Music Club, and more. By encouraging students to participate in extracurricular activities, we believe we are fostering well-rounded individuals who are capable of meeting any challenge with confidence and enthusiasm. At Mahati, we strive to provide our students with a comprehensive education that enables them to reach their full potential and become active, lifelong learners.
