Admission

  • New admissions are given based on availability of seats, admission test and interview of the child.
  • The school Prospectus and admission form is available from the school office in the month of February.
  • Admission to class L.K.G. to class IX are based on the performance of the previous class result and interaction with child and parents.
  • New admissions are given on the basis of availability of seats. Online portal for registration is available on the school website www.inspirationschoolkgm.org from first week of February.
  • Admission to class L.K.G. to class IX and XI are based on the performance of the previous class result and interaction with child and parents.
  • In regard to admission of the child the decision of the Principal will be final.
  • The minimum eligibility for the admission in class Nursery as on 1st April should be 3 years complete.
  • At the time of registration, a photocopy of annual report card of previous school is to be submitted. No child will be admitted without submitting Transfer Certificate from last school attended.
  • At the time of admission following documents are to be submitted with Admission form:

    • (i)  Birth certificate (attested copy)

    (ii)  Transfer certificate of last institution (original)

    (iii)   Latest Photograph-2 (passport size)

    (iv)  Report card of previous school attended (photocopy)

    (iv)  Certified copy of the documents / certificate of category (if any).

Note: Consideration with regard to admission criteria may be given to outstanding students in sports or extra-curricular skills.
