Our History

With a pursuit, to spread the light of quality education among the vicinity of Tadepalligudem, the idea of establishing Mahati School was mooted by the visionaries in 1994. It initially started functioning in the Bandaru’s family home. With 25 students in Primary area, an ancestral home of Bandaru family, 5 teachers and a focussed and firm mentor Mrs. Bala Bandaru as the Founder Chairperson, Mahati’s tryst with destiny began in June, 1994. In Year 2000, the school upgraded till 10th Std. And in March 2002, the school got its first 3-storey building in existing premises. Today Mahati is a proud member of the prestigious educational institutions which is dedicated to providing the best standards in education. Mahati celebrated 10 years of its existence in 2004. Beginning with a humble number of 25 students and 5 teachers, the school has come a long way.

Our Mission

As the world is in a constant state of flux, Mahati recognizes the importance of equipping students with the skills and adaptability necessary to excel in any circumstance. Through an inspiring and challenging educational environment, we aim to cultivate a holistic development approach that encompasses every facet of a student's growth. At Mahati, we are passionate about creating a vibrant and dynamic learning experience that sparks curiosity and fosters a love for lifelong learning. Our ultimate goal is to empower our students with the necessary tools, knowledge, and skills to thrive in a rapidly changing world, and to create a brighter future for themselves and those around them.

Our Vision

Mahati strives to cultivate a dynamic future generation that possesses the ability to envision and create revolutionary ideas of their own.

Founder

As the founder of Mahati School, I am proud to say that our institution is dedicated to providing a world-class education that goes beyond traditional academics. Our aim is to create a learning environment that inspires and challenges students to achieve their full potential and become well-rounded individuals capable of making a positive impact on society.

At Mahati School, we believe that education should not be limited to the classroom, and we provide our students with access to state-of-the-art facilities and a diverse range of extracurricular activities. We strive to foster an inclusive community that values diversity and encourages collaboration, teamwork, and leadership.

Our faculty and staff are committed to providing personalized attention and support to each student, ensuring that they receive the guidance and mentorship they need to succeed. We believe that by providing a holistic and engaging educational experience, we can empower our students to become active, lifelong learners and leaders in their respective fields.

It is my sincere hope that Mahati School will continue to be a beacon of academic excellence and innovation, inspiring future generations to pursue their passions and make a positive impact on the world around them.

Manohar Bandaru, Founder & Secretary
